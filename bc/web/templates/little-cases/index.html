The Big Cases bot does the important work of following the biggest cases in the country, but many people want updates for the topics they follow.

Out of this need, we created the Little Cases bots to follow important topics in American society and jurisprudence.

Little bots are created and manged by Free Law Project, but are curated in partnership with community members or organizations that are experts on particular areas of the law.

If there's a legal topic you follow and if you want to curate a bot for that topic, let us know using the following button. We'll get back to you as soon as possible with a plan for action.
